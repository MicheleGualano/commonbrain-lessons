Background tasks notify on completion — don't poll
#process #agents #harness #efficiency
Rule. When the harness re-invokes you on a background task's completion, do not sleep or loop to poll it — react to the completion notification instead. And remember a zero exit or empty output is not proof of success: read the actual output before trusting it.

Triggers

  • tempted to poll a background job the harness already notifies on completion
  • should I sleep or loop to check a long background build, or wait for the notification
  • a task exited 0 but did not actually do the right thing
  • reacting to a background-task completion notification instead of polling
