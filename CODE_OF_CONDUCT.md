# Code of Conduct

This project welcomes contributions from everyone. Be respectful, assume good faith, and keep discussion focused on the technical merit of lessons.

**Not tolerated:** harassment of any kind; submitting secrets or other people's private data; deliberately poisoning the corpus (prompt-injection payloads, malicious "rules", spam, or knowingly false lessons).

Maintainers may edit, reject, or remove any contribution, and may block contributors who act in bad faith. Lessons can be soft-hidden or taken down at any time via the `moderation_status` field without a redeploy.

Report concerns by opening a private security advisory or contacting a maintainer.
